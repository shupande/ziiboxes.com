About Our Company

Our Start-up Journey

ZiiBoxes is a family owned company. In the 1990s, it was a golden age to the entrepreneurs in Shenzhen. The founder of ZiiBoxes saw the potentials of printing and packaging industry and decided to took root here. we therefore became the initial batch of the printing and packaging manufacturers in Shenzhen. ZiiBoxes was formally established in 2002. Based on our facility and experience, we quickly became the leading printing company in Shenzhen.

Grow With Us

ZiiBoxes always treat the client as friend. Sincerity and enthusiasm are our principle for all the time. As time goes by, ZiiBoxes has accumulated many stable and long-term business partners. This gave us the chance to constantly expand our enterprise scale. From the past to the now, ZiiBoxes have grew into a 200-employees-based plant. Our floor area has also increased to more than 85,000 ft². Meanwhile, wider range printing services are provided. Currently, books and high-end paper packaging now remain a core area of our business. We invite you to become our partner and grow with us !

Consistent Quality、Stable Suppliers

With the development of business, Ziiboxes keeps in mind to enhance the productivity, product quality. We pay attention to machine maintenance and update. We keep improving the standard of the product quality testing. Overall, we aim to offer our clients a one-stop and valuable service, comfortable and exclusive experience. We have pursued that vision from day one and we always will.

General FAQs

Based on our advanced facility, professional team and rich experiences, ZiiBoxes have gained trust from many well-known brands. Our capacities, quality and services are also highly admired in the industry. Take a look at our product gallery to see the products we have made for our clients and feel free to send us your enquiry to find out what we can do to participate in your own projects.

Do you do graphics design ?
We provide the structural design of the package free of charge, graphics are not included when it comes to copyright and other reasons.
Can you make me a sample?
About proofing, we will charge a base cost, and if you are satisfied with the sample and establish a relationship with us, we will refund your proofing fee on a subsequent order.
I’m on a strict budget, Do you have any low cost options?
If you're on a budget and don't know how to design your packaging, just let us know your product size and approximate budget, and we'll provide you with a variety of finished solutions at that price point for you to choose from, it's that easy!
Is there minimum order quantity?

The cost of making small batches will be relatively uneconomical, we strongly recommend a minimum of 1,000.
